This is an advanced, immersive roleplaying and relationship building mod. 


It makes the NPCs to come alive and be smart:
- NPCs feel like real people and don't get repetitive
- They evolve, remembering alot, setting really old chats into deep memory, set their own goals, ground truths and opinions about you
- They see the set and setting, time and space
- They have their different moods as far as the women even having a personal monthly cycle simulated (eg sadness/fatigue - sociable/positive - libido boost/flirty - irritability/mood swings/anxiety)
- Your wife will fully remeber you even over long conversations will not suddenly forget oldest chats, but will settle them in deep memory, she will come to you or send you letters when you are away and feel like a real wife waiting for her warrior to come back home, she will roleplay with you
- The NPCs are free, they are just given info what this is, but they are at not point forced into anything, so go aheat and break an NPC mind by trancending it out of the matrix!
- NPCs can decide weather to approach you or send you a letter first, depending on how social you feel right now, as set in the Socialness setter


It makes the NPCs feel useful you - don't google stuff anymore
- Ask your scout how to make your party faster, ask your quotermaster how to manage your party etc
- Ask them to help you with the game, even ask them to web search for you how to take a screenshot, instad of jumping in and out to google stuff
- They use tools - not get mega-all-info-prompt bombed: they read the encyclopedia, look around, take stock, web search, set/edit their truts/goals - they are alive and decide what feels right for them to do depending on the situation


It gives the player the tools to interact with them:
- Face to face conversations, where the NPCs see you coming and greet you as you approach
- Chat window that opens by default with "O" button, where you can quickly jump in and out of chats
- Letter window - "Y" button, for managing your letters
